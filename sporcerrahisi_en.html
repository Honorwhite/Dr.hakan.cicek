Sports Surgery and Injury Treatment in Adana

Sports surgery in Adana is a specialized field focused on diagnosing and treating injuries to ligaments, cartilage, and tendons in both professional athletes and active individuals. Sports injuries often arise from sudden movements, overexertion, or direct trauma. Common conditions include Anterior Cruciate Ligament (ACL) tears, meniscus injuries, shoulder dislocations, and joint cartilage damage.

Prof. Dr. Hakan Çiçek is a highly experienced orthopedic surgeon specializing in sports medicine in Adana. The primary objective in treating sports injuries is to preserve and repair joint structures, enabling patients to return to their peak performance or active lifestyles as quickly as possible. Modern arthroscopic (minimally invasive) techniques are utilized for optimal results and faster recovery.

What are the advantages of arthroscopic surgery? Performed through tiny incisions, arthroscopy significantly reduces tissue damage. This leads to less postoperative pain, a lower risk of infection, and a much faster healing process compared to traditional open surgery.

Sports surgery services in Adana include:

  • Arthroscopic ACL and other ligament reconstructions.
  • Meniscus repair and suturing.
  • Shoulder arthroscopy (Rotator cuff tears, impingement, recurrent dislocations).
  • Patellar (kneecap) dislocation surgery.
  • Cartilage repair and transplantation (microfracture, mosaicplasty).
Rehabilitation after sports surgery is critical for long-term success. Personalized physical therapy programs restore muscle strength and joint range of motion. Prof. Dr. Hakan Çiçek closely monitors his patients throughout their recovery to ensure a safe and effective return to sports.

If you’ve suffered a sports injury or have persistent joint pain, schedule a consultation with Prof. Dr. Hakan Çiçek in Adana to prevent further damage and start your journey back to fitness today.

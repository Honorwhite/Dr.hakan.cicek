Orthopedic Trauma and Modern Treatment Methods

Orthopedic trauma treatment includes modern surgical techniques used in the treatment of fractures, dislocations, soft tissue injuries, and traumatic joint damage. Thanks to minimally invasive approaches and advanced technologies, patients have the chance to recover and return to daily life in a shorter time. Prof. Dr. Hakan Çiçek achieves high success rates in orthopedic trauma treatment with personalized treatment planning and modern surgical techniques.

Fracture Treatment

  • • Minimally Invasive Fracture Surgery
  • • Internal Fixation
  • • External Fixation
  • • Intramedullary Nailing
  • • Plate and Screw Systems

Dislocation and Joint Injuries

  • • Acute Dislocation Treatment
  • • Intra-articular Fracture Treatment
  • • Soft Tissue Repair

Sports Injuries

Conditions Requiring Orthopedic Trauma

  • • Sports injuries
  • • Work accidents
  • • Injuries due to falls and impacts
  • • High-energy traumas

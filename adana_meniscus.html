Adana Meniscus Treatment

Adana meniscus treatment - You are in the right place for the most up-to-date and comprehensive information. The meniscus is a cartilage structure in the knee joint that acts as a shock absorber and ensures the harmony of joint surfaces. Sports injuries, sudden twisting movements, aging, and trauma are the most common causes of meniscus tears. Meniscus tears present with symptoms such as knee pain, swelling, limited movement, and locking. The risk of meniscus tears is higher in athletes, active individuals, and the elderly.

In the field of meniscus treatment in Adana, Prof. Dr. Hakan Çiçek offers his patients modern diagnostic methods (MRI, clinical examination) and personalized treatment options. Treatment is planned according to the type of tear, the patient's age, activity level, and expectations. For mild tears, rest, medication, and physical therapy are applied, while in advanced cases, arthroscopic meniscus surgery is performed for repair or meniscectomy using minimally invasive techniques. Arthroscopic surgery is performed through small incisions, resulting in less pain, lower risk of infection, and a shorter recovery period.

After meniscus surgery in Adana, patients undergo a rehabilitation program with expert physiotherapists. The aim is to strengthen the knee, maintain range of motion, and ensure a quick return to daily life. Early diagnosis and the right approach are critical for protecting knee health.

Symptoms of Meniscus Tear

  • • Sudden knee pain and swelling
  • • Limited movement
  • • Locking or catching sensation in the knee
  • • Difficulty climbing stairs

Meniscus Treatment Methods

  • • Physical therapy and rest
  • • Medication
  • • Arthroscopic meniscus surgery
  • • Meniscus repair and suturing
  • • Meniscus removal (meniscectomy)
